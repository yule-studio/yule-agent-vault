- [Adobe Color](https://color.adobe.com/) — Adobe 컬러 wheel
- [CSS Color Module 4](https://drafts.csswg.org/css-color-4/) — oklch / lab 색 공간
- [Open Color](https://yeun.github.io/open-color/) — open-source 컬러 시스템
- [A11y color tools](https://webaim.org/resources/contrastchecker/) — 대비 검사
- [Dark mode 가이드 (Material)](https://m3.material.io/styles/color/dark-theme) — 다크 모드
- [Inclusive color (Trevor Strunk)](https://www.smashingmagazine.com/2021/04/inclusive-design-mistakes/) — 포용적 컬러
- [Korean typography 가이드](https://www.smashingmagazine.com/2018/11/text-layout-css-grid-flexbox/) — 한국어 타이포
